# Skill: chile-market-map

## When to invoke

Any agent that needs to:

- pick which boards or headhunters to query for a given role
- enrich a job posting with company-context (sector dynamics, ownership, scale)
- decide whether a given employer matches the candidate's target sectors
- map a target role to likely warm-intro paths

## Patterns to remember

- The Chilean executive market is **small and relationship-driven**. Above
  Senior PM / Director level, ~60–70% of placements are referral or
  headhunter — not posted. Outreach > applications.
- Public companies report to **CMF** (cmfchile.cl); financials and
  ownership changes are public and useful for company research.
- Many startups list comp in **CLP gross monthly** (not annual). A "$5M"
  posting means CLP 5,000,000/mo gross.
- Gratificación legal: ~4.75 monthly salaries/yr extra (capped). When
  comparing to USD comp, gross-annualize CLP * 13 (approx).
- "Gerente" ≈ Director (US). "Subgerente" ≈ Senior Manager. "Jefe" ≈
  Manager. Mind the inflation when mapping seniority from US titles.
- Two main hiring waves: **March–May** (post-summer) and
  **September–November**. December–February is dead for executive search.
