# Skill: interview-pm-latam

## When to invoke

- Interview Prep agent is building STAR stories / case warmups.
- Any sub-agent asked about likely-questions for a CL/Latam product role.
- The Tailor agent needs to know which CV bullets resonate with which
  hiring culture (corporate vs scale-up vs marketplace).

## Hiring-culture archetypes

| Archetype | Examples | What they probe most |
|---|---|---|
| **Corporate / retail conglomerate** | Falabella, Cencosud, Ripley, Banco de Chile, BCI | Stakeholder management, structured prioritization, "matrix politics", ability to operate inside large committees |
| **Latam scale-up / unicorn** | Mercado Libre, NotCo, Cornershop, Cabify, Globant | Speed, ownership, willingness to break process, metric obsession, ambiguity |
| **Vertical SaaS / B2B** | Buk, Defontana, Toku, Xepelin, Khipu | Discovery rigor, time-to-value, sales-led vs PLG, churn analytics |
| **Mission / impact** | Betterfly, Crehana, NotCo (sustainability angle), Pegas con Sentido portfolio | Why-this-mission, theory of change, dual-bottom-line tradeoffs |
| **Mining / energy / utilities** | Codelco, Enel, Engie, Copec | Risk management, regulatory navigation, multi-year roadmaps, on-site ops literacy |

## Behavioral themes that almost always come up

- **0-to-1 / launch under ambiguity** — especially at scale-ups and at
  Copec/Falabella's innovation arms. The candidate should have one tight
  story of going from idea → funded → live in <12 months.
- **Killing your own project** — failure / pivot story. Latam interviewers
  test for honest reflection, not just "we shipped and learned."
- **Stakeholder conflict** — at corporates, expect "manager vs. CFO" or
  "comercial vs. operaciones". Demonstrate using data and a structured
  framework to resolve, not personal charisma alone.
- **Data-driven decision** — be specific about the metric, the threshold
  for action, and what you'd have done if the data said the opposite.

## PM-functional question patterns

Prioritization:
  - "Tienes 3 iniciativas y solo recursos para 1. ¿Cómo decides?"
  - Frameworks they like: RICE, weighted-shortest-job-first, ICE. Avoid
    "I just go with gut" answers.

Metrics:
  - "¿Qué métrica define el éxito de tu producto?" Expect a follow-up:
    "¿Y la métrica que más te preocupa que NO esté creciendo?"
  - For marketplaces (ML, Cornershop): liquidity, take-rate, cohort retention.
  - For B2B SaaS (Buk, Defontana): MRR, gross retention, NRR, time-to-value,
    activation rate.

Roadmap / vision:
  - "Dibújame el roadmap de 12 meses si fueras dueño de este producto."
  - At Mercado Libre / NotCo this becomes a true mini-case: 30-45 min.

Cross-functional:
  - "Cuéntame una vez que tuviste que decirle no a Ingeniería / a Ventas /
    al CEO." Test for spine + diplomacy.

## Case-warmup patterns by archetype

**Retail / marketplace**
> "Lanza un nuevo vertical de [pet supplies / electrónica usada] dentro de
> [Falabella.com / Mercado Libre]. ¿Cómo defines MVP, métricas y riesgo?"

Framework: Customer segments → core JTBD → unit economics → supply onboarding
→ trust/quality → growth loop.

**Vertical SaaS**
> "Buk quiere reducir churn de PYMEs en el primer trimestre. ¿Por dónde
> partes?"

Framework: cohort segmentation by use-case → activation milestones →
intervention points (onboarding, CSM trigger, in-product nudge) →
measurement (control vs treatment).

**Mission-driven / impact**
> "Betterfly quiere expandirse a un segmento B2C individual sin canibalizar
> el B2B. ¿Cómo lo estructuras?"

Framework: brand positioning conflict → pricing tiers → onboarding flow →
distribution channel choice → success metric (engagement, not vanity).

## Questions a senior PM should credibly ask

- "¿Cómo se mide el éxito de este rol a los 6 meses? ¿Cuál sería la
  decisión que más rápido tendría que tomar?"
- "¿Qué iniciativa actual está más atrasada respecto al plan, y por qué?"
- "¿Cómo es la cadencia de revisión con el [CEO / VP de Producto]?"
- "¿Qué decisiones de producto NO toma el PM en este equipo?"
- "¿Cuál fue la última hipótesis de producto que se invalidó? ¿Cómo lo
  procesaron?"
